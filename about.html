Frequently Asked Questions

Q: What is the origin of your name?
A: It’s a silly story. When I came back to CS:GO right before CS2, I was using “Chicken” with a chicken profile picture—basically a shitpost. In a game on Cache, the enemy team had coordinated names like “Horse on a Balcony,” “Horse on a Car,” etc. I wanted to join in, searched for “Chicken on a Horse,” found an image, and switched my name and avatar. The rest is history (and I later had a custom profile picture made).
Q: What do you do for work?

Q: How long have you been playing CS?
A: I started playing CS:GO in 2017.
Q: How long have you been collecting?
A: I first collected in 2017, played again in 2020, and started building my current collection in mid-2023.
Q: What is your favorite skin that you own?
